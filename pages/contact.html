Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I receive a response? ▼
We typically respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. For urgent technical issues, we aim to respond within 12 hours. Premium members receive priority support with even faster response times.
Can I schedule a demo or consultation? ▼
Absolutely! We offer free 30-minute consultation calls for educational institutions, and team plans. Select "Partnership Inquiry" in the contact form above, or email us directly at hello@edusync.com to schedule your demo.
How do I report a bug or technical issue? ▼
Select "Bug Report" from the subject dropdown in the contact form. Please include as much detail as possible: what you were doing when the issue occurred, what happened, error messages if any, and what browser/device you're using. Screenshots are also very helpful!
Is my data secure and private? ▼
Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and security practices to protect your data. Your personal information is never shared with third parties for marketing purposes. All data is stored securely, and we comply with international data protection regulations including GDPR. For more details, please review our Privacy Policy.
What languages does EduSync support? ▼
EduSync currently supports English, Arabic,and french . We're constantly working to add more languages. If you'd like to see a specific language added, please let us know through the contact form!
Can I request new features? ▼
Yes! We love hearing from our users and many of our best features came from user suggestions. Select "Feature Request" in the contact form and tell us about your idea. We review all suggestions and prioritize them based on user feedback and feasibility.
Do you offer phone support? ▼
Currently, we provide support through email and our help center.
